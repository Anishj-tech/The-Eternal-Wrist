The Philosophy

The Eternal Wrist was born from a singular conviction: that a watch is not merely a device for measuring time, but a philosophical statement about how one chooses to live within it.

Founded in Geneva in 1987 by master horologist Arnaud Leclerc, our maison has spent four decades curating the world's most exceptional timepieces — selecting only those that embody the highest standards of mechanical artistry and enduring design.

We do not chase trends. We identify permanence. Every piece in our collection has been chosen to be as relevant in fifty years as it is today.
